# Storecraft

## About Us

Storecraft is a comprehensive, global project management consultancy specializing in the retail and hospitality sectors. We excel in managing the entire store development process, from site feasibility analysis to store opening, allowing brands to focus on delivering exceptional customer experiences.

With expert teams in Paris and London, our services encompass site risk analysis, permitting and approvals, lease negotiations, vendor sourcing, cost management, capital expenditure optimization, scheduling, procurement, project delivery, and strategic process implementation.

At Storecraft, we take full ownership of every project, ensuring seamless execution and outstanding results.
